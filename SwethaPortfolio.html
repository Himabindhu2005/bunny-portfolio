1.2.3 Cultural Program

Participating in the Bathukamma cultural program at my college was a joyful and memorable experience. Bathukamma, the traditional floral festival of Telangana, celebrates nature, culture, and the spirit of womanhood. Students came together dressed in traditional attire, arranged beautiful flower stacks, sang folk songs, and danced around the Bathukamma. The event promoted cultural awareness, unity, and respect for our rich heritage while creating a vibrant and festive atmosphere on campus.

1.2.4 Swachh Bharat

Participating in the Swachh Bharat activity at my college was a meaningful and responsible experience. It made me realize the importance of cleanliness and maintaining a healthy environment. Through the activity, I actively contributed to keeping the campus clean and developed a sense of civic responsibility. It also encouraged teamwork and awareness about hygiene among students.
